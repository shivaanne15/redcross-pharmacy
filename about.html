About Red Cross Pharmacy

An independent, locally owned pharmacy in the heart of Eureka — where every patient is a neighbor.

Our story

Pharmacy the way it should be

Red Cross Pharmacy was built on a simple belief: getting your medications shouldn't feel like standing in line at a warehouse. When you walk through our doors at 525 5th Street, you're greeted by a team that knows your name, understands your health history, and genuinely cares about how you're doing.

As an independent community pharmacy, we answer to our patients — not to corporate shareholders. That means shorter waits, straight answers, and the flexibility to go the extra mile when you need it.

We're proud to serve Eureka and the wider Humboldt County community, and we work hard every day to earn the trust our neighbors place in us.
